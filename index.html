About me

I'm Umakant Yadav, a Full Stack Web Developer and an aspiring Data Analyst from Surat, Gujarat. I work primarily with Java, Python, PHP, JavaScript libraries, Bootstrap, MySQL and more in various projects. I enjoy turning complex problems into simple, robust and maintainable solutions.

My job is to build your website so that it is functional and user-friendly but at the same time attractive. Moreover, I add personal touch to your product and make sure that is eye-catching and easy to use. My aim is to bring across your message and identity in the most creative way. I created web design for many famous brand companies. My work includes building dynamic admin panels with DataTables, generating bulk QR codes and ZIP export tools, integrating OpenCV (face detection & crop) with PHP, and building PHP → Python bridges for Indic NLP transliteration. I also experiment with data workflows and analytics to move towards Data Analyst roles.
